TraceVeda – Ayurvedic Herb Traceability & Supply Chain Management Software

TraceVeda is an advanced blockchain-based platform specifically designed for the Ayurvedic herbs industry. Our platform addresses critical challenges in the supply chain by providing complete transparency and traceability from farm to consumer.

Our solution integrates advanced blockchain technology with sophisticated IoT sensors to real-time monitoring of temperature, humidity, and other environmental conditions throughout the supply chain process.

Whether you're a farmer, manufacturer, distributor, retailer, or lab, TraceVeda connects all stakeholders in the Ayurvedic herbs ecosystem to ensure the highest standards of herbal processing, testing, and quality management.
